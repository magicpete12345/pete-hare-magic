★ Hello there ★

A magician kids actually remember

Pete Hare has spent years learning how to hold a roomful of children spellbound — from the wide-eyed three-year-olds at the front to the "I'm-too-cool-for-this" nine-year-olds at the back. Spoiler: by the end, everyone's joining in.

Based near Woking and performing across Guildford, Cobham, Weybridge, Chobham, Sunningdale and surrounding Surrey villages, Pete brings proper magic, lots of laughter, plenty of audience participation, and a show that grown-ups enjoy just as much as the kids. No cringey gags. No tired routines. Just genuine astonishment, wrapped up in a brilliant party.

★ The fee ★

Where your fee goes

100% to charity

Pete has a full-time day job. Magic is his way of giving back — so every penny of his fee is donated to a charity. You're welcome to suggest a cause when you book; Pete is always happy to talk it through.

Fees are commercial — proportional to experience and skill level — and Pete won't knowingly undercut other local magicians.
